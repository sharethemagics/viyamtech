CHANNEL MARKETING MANAGEMENT

Why Channel Marketing?

There was a time when companies solely relied on traditional marketing media, such as word-of-mouth. But, with increased incorporation of web-based technologies, change is in the air. Additionally, companies are automating their interactions with clients at all levels. The rise of social networks has seen companies digitize dealings with clients. This is where our Channel Marketing portfolio can be a significant tool to assist companies.

CONTENT BUILDING

We understand that marketing content has an important role in a multi-channel marketing strategy. High-impact content that explains the product portfolio, service features as well as the company’s objectives is necessary. Our qualified team that has a strong background across industrial sectors can help create effective content.

SOCIAL MEDIA RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

In the contemporary business environment, social media touches everything from product development and sales and marketing to customer service. Viyam Technologies believes in working with clients from stage one and contributing to efficient research and analysis. Our team can monitor social media conversations about a client’s product and brand, analyze those interactions and trends, and provide customized research reports that help clients engage effectively with their customers.
