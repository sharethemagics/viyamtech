VIYAM Technology -

A Research, Analytical, and Advisory Marketing Company

Viyam Technologies is a research, analysis and advisory marketing company that helps reach your products and services to prospective consumers. Gaining meaningful, actionable marketing insights from varied consumer-centric data spread across multiple platforms, including social media, is an arduous task.

Plus, in today’s technology-rich times, engagement with consumers, be it in the form of email, Mobile or Social Media, works better when automated. But, we believe the human interface is important too. What our team does is use advanced tools to track, research, analyse and generate consumer data from various platforms. Because, a human mind is behind it, the data is specific to a client’s needs, and not something auto-generated.

This data can be utilized to maximize the value of a client’s campaigns, including marketing. With effective campaigns, we can assist you in achieving intelligent growth, and overall organizational success.

Why Our Company?

The efficient tools and related methodology we deploy generate sound data, and our experienced analysts can interpret data innovatively, and customize it based on your needs. We also offer personalized assessment and solutions so that any specific issues clients raise can be dealt with to their satisfaction.

Web intelligence and real-time tracking of leads are important for businesses, and we aid in that process too. Even if you do have a digital marketing strategy, but not the optimal resources or time to actualize it, or if you need an external perspective, get in touch with us. Our experts will work with your marketing team and assist it to achieve organizational goals.
